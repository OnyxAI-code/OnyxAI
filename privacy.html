Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

OnyxAI Solutions LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit onyxaisolutions.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you fill out our contact form:

  • Name and email address
  • Company name
  • Message content

We also collect usage data automatically through Google Analytics, including pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/city level).

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule discovery calls
  • To analyze website traffic and improve our site
  • To communicate about our services

Cookies

We use cookies for Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. You can decline cookies using the banner on our homepage. You can also disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for reasonable business record-keeping purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact us at contact@onyxaisolutions.com.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@onyxaisolutions.com.
